Oh The Places I Will Go

Have you ever seen pictures of beautiful places and thought to yourself I am going to go there one day. I know I have.
Throughout the years, I have looked at pictures of alluring cities, landscapes and countries and I've made a mental
note to myself that I will venture off to see these places one day. I just know I will!
I always felt like a bird trapped inside a cage, I felt I was never meant to be trapped, but meant to be Free!


This world of ours is so spectacular, I just don't believe we are meant to stay in one place forever.

Over the years, I have collected a list of places that I would like to visit.
I would like to share a little bit of that list with you. I guess you can call it My Travel Bucket List.
